About

"Here are some introductory facts so you may be able to know me just a little bit better."


I grew up in Washington, Alaska, and South Dakota.

I lived for almost nine years in Washington after I was born there, where I learned to love apples and rain quite a bit (you kind of have to). My family and I moved to Alaska so my father could take ownership of my grandfather's fuel company in a small town called Seldovia (less than three-hundred people). After about seven years in Alaska our family took an ETT (Emergency Trauma Technician) course together and my dad became passionate about medicine, so he went to Sioux Falls temporarily to pursure paramedicine. He liked it so much here that he told us to visit and see how we like it, and the rest is history.

My family often spent time in Mexico every year.

When we were in Alaska, we would go down to Mexico every year for at least a month. I went to school down there twice and the high school was one of the best experiences of my life. We didn't have a car so we would walk every day. We would walk to the beach about every other day and be there until the sun set.
